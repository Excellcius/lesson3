Target Audience

Our target audience is composed of business owners and professionals in the Point of the Mountain area. We also aim to reach individuals and families in the community who are interested in supporting local businesses.

Personas

  • John, Small Business Owner

    John is a 35-year-old small business owner in the Point of the Mountain area. He owns a local hardware store and is actively involved in the community. He is looking for resources and networking opportunities to help grow his business.

  • Samantha, Community Member

    Samantha is a 28-year-old working professional who recently moved to the Point of the Mountain area. She is actively involved in the community and is interested in supporting local businesses. She is looking for information on events and deals in the area.

Scenarios

  • John is searching for networking opportunities to connect with other local business owners.
  • Samantha is looking for information on upcoming community events and deals at local businesses.
  • John is in need of resources to help grow his business and is looking for information on local business resources.
  • Samantha is interested in getting involved in the community and is searching for volunteer opportunities through local businesses.
  • John is looking for information on how to become a member of the Point of the Mountain Chamber.
